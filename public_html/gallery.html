Tropical Rain Forests

Rain forest is simply an area of tall, mostly evergreen trees and a high amount of rainfall. Sri Lanka is gifted with a rain forest where it is considered as a wonder of the world. Rain forests are the Earth s oldest living eco systems and Sinharaja forest is one of the greatest things that Sri Lankans have. It is our duty to protect this wonder of the world.

Sigiriya

Sigiriya which is known as the eighth wonder of the world is one of the most valuable historical monuments of Sri Lanka. This is an ancient palace and fortress complex has significant archaeological importance and this attracts thousands of tourists every year. Sigiriya rock plateau formed from the magma of an extinct volcano that has been surrounded by the jungles.
